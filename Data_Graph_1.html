eCPMs by Ad and Regions




           All ad placements on Zedge are not created equal as seen from this overview. The Medium Rectangle (MREC) ecpm outperforms by 2 times the amount of all other placements across each Country Region (except the Native Item ad placement in English Speaking countries). Due to the MREC success, Zedge will look at alternative solutions to implement the MREC and replace applicable Native ads which are similar in size.

    NOTE: ecpm = revenue per thousand (a standardized KPI metric in advertising)
